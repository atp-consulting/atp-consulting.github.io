Our Services

Process Audits & Debottlenecking

We identify capacity limits, energy losses, and inefficiencies through targeted audits. Our team has experience resolving bottlenecks in distillation columns, SRU systems, and amine units — improving throughput and stability without major capex. This approach is applicable across a wide range of plant units and configurations.

Process Control Optimization

We tune control strategies to reduce variability and improve responsiveness. This includes optimizing control of fired heaters, fractionation columns, or surge-sensitive systems. Whether it's basic PID or advanced logic, we adapt to your control platform and process requirements.

Instrumentation Diagnostics

We evaluate sensor accuracy, signal noise, and installation issues affecting plant performance. Typical cases include unreliable level or flow measurements in columns, exchangers, or tank systems. We work across all key instrumentation — ensuring the control system sees what’s really happening.

Digital Transformation Strategy

We evaluate your plant’s data readiness to design a practical digital roadmap. From analytics to digital twins, we help define what's feasible and useful — no buzzwords, just real results grounded in engineering reality.
